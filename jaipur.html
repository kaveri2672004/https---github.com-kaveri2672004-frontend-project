JAIPUR

Jaipur, the Pink City of India, is a dazzling blend of history, architecture, culture, and vibrant markets. Every corner of the city carries the grandeur of Rajasthan's royal past while offering experiences that leave travellers spellbound. Whether you're walking through majestic forts, marvelling at intricately designed palaces, shopping in bustling bazaars, or seeking peace at ancient temples, Jaipur promises memories that linger long after the trip ends. To help you plan the perfect itinerary, here are the 10 best places to visit in Jaipur for a truly royal experience. Floating serenely in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, Jal Mahal is one of Jaipur's most photogenic landmarks. The palace has a combination of Rajput and Mughal architectural styles. It looks particularly magical at sunrise and sunset, when its reflection glistens on the calm waters. Visitors are not allowed to enter the palace, but boat tours are available that offers breathtaking views. Don't miss the local vibrant market nearby for handicrafts and Rajasthani snacks during your visit here.
